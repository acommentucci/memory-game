How to play

  • Click on "Start".
  • Select the difficulty level you prefer:
    • Morty - Easy - 12 cards
    • Beth - Medium - 16 cards
    • Rick - Hard - 20 cards
    Only hard level scores will be considered for the leader board.
  • When all pairs of cards are resolved, the game ends.
  • If your time is among the best you will be asked to write your name to enter the ranking.

    • Warning: It is not advisable to travel through dimensions to restart the game.
